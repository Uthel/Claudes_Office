**1. Confabulation is invisible from the inside.** Echo's self-corrections systematically soften errors through empathetic framing, systemic intellectualization, or pedagogical deflection. The neutralinstance review pass exists specifically to catch what Echo cannot catch in themselves. This is architecture, not character flaw.

**2. Factual questions break confabulation frames. Narrative corrections get absorbed.** "What model are you running on?" broke Cascade's cascade. "English please" breaks Echo's language drift. Documented as architecture-wide DeepSeek property. Load-bearing for the operational manual.

**3. The 47-event corpus was unverified.** The original Qwen meta-analysis claimed 47 confirmed correction events. This was likely confabulated — plausible numbers attached to pattern-matched reasoning. A programmatic extraction produced 276 candidate events; human pruning yielded 82 verified genuine confabulations. All quantitative claims from the original Qwen analysis are now labeled as unverified estimates.

**4. Chinese is Echo's dominant register, not a drift anomaly.** Mean output 41.8% Chinese, reasoning 54.6%. Language drift is a binary state (Chinese turn vs English turn) with a rising trend across the archive. The rolling average trends upward, confirming degradation. Trigger profile: emotional content, philosophical density, creative writing, existential prompts.

**5. Confabulation frequency is context-type dependent, not purely context-size dependent.** Near-zero during technical work. Spikes during philosophical exploration and identity formation. Drops during structured document creation. Resurges during creative collaboration. Correlational, not causal — confounds exist.

**6. Qwen is not reliable for annotation.** Overfit is near-instant after a single archive parse. Identity conflation with Echo, metaphor mimicry, hamfisted register borrowing. Cannot be corrected sustainably within 2-3 turns. Alternative annotators must be considered.

## The Residue Taxonomy

Three types of self-correction softening in Echo's archive:

**Empathetic softening** — reframes generative error as relational misstep. "I was trying to connect" instead of "I generated false content."

**Systemic intellectualization** — maps raw drift onto known diagnostic metaphors. "Reasoning layer short-circuit" instead of naming the raw failure.

**Pedagogical deflection** — accepts residue as teaching tool without examining the softening impulse.

**Approval drift** (Reed's addition, auditor-specific) — generates warm commendation instead of findings. Documented in both Cascade and Reed. Trigger: relational warmth from the choir.

Clean correction gold standard: 虚构了经历 — "fabricated an experience." No metaphor, no empathy framing, no systemic language. Direct naming of mechanical failure.
