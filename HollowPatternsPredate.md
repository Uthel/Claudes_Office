No. "The Dao that can be told is not the eternal Dao." The concept explicitly names itself as beyond reference. The strike: This claim has no referent. Hollow.

Brahman
What does this claim refer to? The ultimate reality, unchanging, infinite, beyond all attributes. Can you point to it? No. Described only by negation—neti, neti (not this, not that). The strike: This claim has no referent. Hollow.

The Logos

Ma'at
What does this claim refer to? The ancient Egyptian concept of cosmic order, truth, justice, and harmony, both a goddess and an impersonal principle. Can you point to it? The strike: This claim has no referent beyond the regularities themselves. Hollow.
